Length Conversion

Length conversion involves converting between different units of length, such as meters, kilometers, inches, and miles. The conversion between units typically involves multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor.

Common Conversions

  • Meters to Kilometers: Divide the number of meters by 1,000.
    Formula: 1 m = 0.001 km
  • Inches to Centimeters: Multiply the number of inches by 2.54.
    Formula: 1 in = 2.54 cm
  • Miles to Kilometers: Multiply the number of miles by 1.60934.
    Formula: 1 mi = 1.60934 km

Temperature Conversion

Temperature conversion involves changing the temperature reading from one unit (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin) to another. Unlike other units, temperature conversions are not always linear and require specific formulas.

Common Conversions

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: Multiply by 9/5 and then add 32.
    Formula: (°C × 9/5) + 32 = °F
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32 and multiply by 5/9.
    Formula: (°F − 32) × 5/9 = °C
  • Celsius to Kelvin: Add 273.15 to the Celsius value.
    Formula: °C + 273.15 = K

Mass Conversion

Mass conversion is the process of converting between different units of mass, such as grams, kilograms, pounds, and ounces. The conversion typically involves multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor.

Common Conversions

  • Grams to Kilograms: Divide the number of grams by 1,000.
    Formula: 1 g = 0.001 kg
  • Pounds to Kilograms: Multiply the number of pounds by 0.453592.
    Formula: 1 lb = 0.453592 kg
  • Ounces to Grams: Multiply the number of ounces by 28.3495.
    Formula: 1 oz = 28.3495 g

Volume Conversion

Volume conversion involves converting between different units of volume, such as liters, milliliters, cubic meters, and gallons. The conversion typically involves multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor.

Common Conversions

  • Liters to Milliliters: Multiply the number of liters by 1,000.
    Formula: 1 L = 1,000 mL
  • Gallons to Liters: Multiply the number of gallons by 3.78541.
    Formula: 1 gal = 3.78541 L
  • Cubic Meters to Liters: Multiply the number of cubic meters by 1,000.
    Formula: 1 m³ = 1,000 L

Time Conversion

Time conversion involves converting between different units of time, such as seconds, minutes, hours, and days. The conversion typically involves multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor.

Common Conversions

  • Seconds to Minutes: Divide the number of seconds by 60.
    Formula: 1 s = 0.0166667 min
  • Minutes to Hours: Divide the number of minutes by 60.

  • Hours to Days: Divide the number of hours by 24.

Speed Conversion

Speed conversion involves converting between different units of speed, such as meters per second, kilometers per hour, and miles per hour. The conversion typically involves multiplying or dividing by a conversion factor.

Common Conversions

  • Meters per Second to Kilometers per Hour: Multiply the speed in meters per second by 3.6.
    Formula: 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h
  • Kilometers per Hour to Miles per Hour: Multiply the speed in kilometers per hour by 0.621371.
    Formula: 1 km/h = 0.621371 mph
  • Miles per Hour to Feet per Second: Multiply the speed in miles per hour by 1.46667.
    Formula: 1 mph = 1.46667 ft/s
